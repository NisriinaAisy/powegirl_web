HOW TO SURVIVE

To strengthen yourself against bullying, focus on building self-confidence by recognizing your strengths and embracing who you are. Surround yourself with supportive friends and people who uplift you, as a strong social network can help shield you from negativity. Practice assertiveness by calmly standing up for yourself when needed, but avoid escalating conflict. Learn to manage your emotions, stay calm under pressure, and remember that the bully's words or actions do not define your worth. Lastly, seek help from trusted individuals or counselors when necessary, as speaking out can provide valuable support and guidance.
